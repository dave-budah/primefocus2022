Purse Wallet

Purse is a new way to transact. It allows customers to easily fund their brokerage accounts. Currently, Purse is partnered with Binary.com as a payment agent. It looks to add more brokers in time.

UnoPay

UnoPay is a platform to allow easy application for OneMoney offerings such agent codes, merchant codes. It removes the hustle of the paper based system by allowing ordinary Zimbabweans to apply for OneMoney products and services.

Prime Roll

This is a payroll system targeting small to medium enterprises. It allows payments of wages using Mobile money platforms and low value bank accounts.

A brief history about us

Prime Focus Private Limited Zimbabwe is a wholly owned Zimbabwean company which was founded on the principle of financial freedom and consciousness for every Zimbabwean and African at large. It was incorporated in 2018 and has been working on innovative financial solutions. We have payroll and payment aggregation systems under development and almost ready for rollout.
